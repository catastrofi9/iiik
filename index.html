About us

Rocky Robotics data is transparent, trustworthy, and decentralizedly kept on the Solana blockchain. Our goal is to develop a truly decentralized GameFi game with a big market. We want to connect people from all over the world through PVP Robot battling and work together to create a world of economic success, with additional features on the way.

Roadmap

Foundation

⚪ Growth of the Community Begin assembling a devoted following to assist us in making every part of Rocky Robots the best it can be.
⚪ Whitelisting We will reward our early and active members with whitelists during our minting and marketing campaign.

Robotics

⚪ The public mint of 2222 Rocky Robot NFTs; your own unique, configurable avatar to be utilized in our Metavese system.
⚪Listings on the Marketplace/Rarities After we have sold out, we will publish on Magic Eden for our chosen secondary market, giving consumers a place to sell their Robots.

Metaverse

⚪ Duel (PVP) is ongoing in collaboration with the community, with weekly updates and a beta-net test run beginning in mid-June 2022, with expected full release July 2022
⚪ Following the debut of PVP, Battle Mode (PvE) is in full development, prioritizing user experience in our metaverse maps, with a private beta to beta testers starting in July and a public version launching in January 2023.

Ascent

⚪ Upgrades to our game assets and user experience to improve gameplay, such as map expansion and extra accessories, PVE expansion, and more general upgrades to stay current.
